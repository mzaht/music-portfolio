Splish Splash

Original composition by me

This is an uptempo swing chart which starts off the piece with the band coming in with high energy in each instrument. Following the intro, we go into a AABA Rhythm Changes form where the saxes take over the melody at A. The B section involves all horns playing counterpoint which is then resolved with the saxes ending it, going onto the last A section. The trumpets take over the melody, but it suddenly disappears with the trombones building up the energy for the upcoming sax soli right after. After that, the 2nd trumpet takes the first solo followed by the 1st Tenor, which leads into the shout chorus where the ensemble trades with the drums.
